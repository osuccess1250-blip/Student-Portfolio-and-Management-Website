Academic Planner

Stay On Track

Add assignments, deadlines, and study goals. Tasks are saved automatically and will still be here when you come back.

How It Works

  • Type a task, pick a priority, and click Add Task.
  • Click the checkbox to mark a task as done.
  • Click X to delete a task.
  • Tasks are saved in your browser and survive page refreshes.
  • Use the filters to view All, Pending, or Done tasks.
